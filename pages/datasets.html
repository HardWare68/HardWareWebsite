Datasets

Lots of datasets that I have gathered over my time! Many of them are video-game-centric since I enjoy playing games, but there are a few non-video-game datasets here.

CSV files may be downloaded as a .zip file due to the multiple sheets. In such a case, try a direct link to the file, such as website.com/datasets/csv/CounterStrike2/[dataset].csv.

For several datasets, they may be the data that was used in a video of mine. In a case like that, it will usually be a subset of the original sheet it came from.
